# Blockchain Technology Solution

## Solution Architecture

The architecture proposed is essential for enabling secure, equitable, and responsible access to humanity's data, as well as for providing secure storage and transmission of data, protecting privacy and data security, and enabling the development of new technologies and applications. Moreover, this architecture is important for fostering collaboration, innovation, and public trust in the use of this data.

The architecture will consist of 3 main layers: Data/Storage layer, Application/Consensus layer and Consumer Layer.
